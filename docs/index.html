Fellowship of the Fervent (“FoF”) is a Christian men's ministry that serves the community by building up, connecting, and mobilizing men and men's ministries for the expansion of God's kingdom.

We provide a safe space for fellowship, encouragement, biblical teaching, life-on-life discipleship, and fervent prayer.

We meet in person every Saturday morning at 7am at one of three Panera Bread locations in the Atlanta metro. We also gather for special events and 2–3 conferences each year.

Our prayer is to be a place where men and men's ministries can connect, share, collaborate, and grow in Christ.

What We Do

Four pillars. One mission.

Everything we do is in service of building up, connecting, and mobilizing men for the kingdom of God.

Fellowship

Brothers gathering with brothers — sharing real life over the Word and the table.

Discipleship

Life-on-life formation rooted in Scripture, sharpened in community, lived out daily.

Fervent Prayer

Persistent, unashamed prayer for our families, our city, and the global church.

Mobilization

Equipping men and men's ministries to serve, lead, and expand the kingdom.
